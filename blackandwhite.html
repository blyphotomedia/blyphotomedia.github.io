Black & White Series

Black & White Series Â© 2009

Selection of images from traditional black and white darkroom prints to digital black and white prints.

Methodology- using traditional black and white 35mm film, on a Canon SLR camera, exposed two images onto one frame. Developed the prints in the darkroom using Fibre Based photographic paper.

The last image is a digital photograph using a Canon DSLR, postproduction done in Photoshop, to alter the colour to B&W. The image is part of a series based on a fictional crime scene.
