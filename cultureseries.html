Culture Series (2011)

Selection of images from the Culture Series, exploring the concept on whether 'Two Cultures Can Become Intimate?'.

As a British Born Chinese living in England, I have grown up with two cultures; the aim here was to portray my own interpretation on how I have intertwined the Chinese & British cultures, into a mixed culture, that has helped defined my identity.
