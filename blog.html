Blog & Research

This page will be dedicated to exploring the links between 'Trauma and Creativity' whether the two have a role in how one uses their trauama to create artworks.

There has been research to suggest there is a direct link, as part of the brain is accessed when one experiences trauma. However I want to explore this link further by exploring how surrealism artworks uses symbolism as a form of expression, works by Salvador Dali etc.

The research will consist of psychology based research, along with theories by Sigmund Freud, Carl Jung along with many others.

PTSD will be a foundation into exploring the links between trauma and creativity, along with neuroscientific reasearch into the processes of the brain such as the fight/flight/freeze.
